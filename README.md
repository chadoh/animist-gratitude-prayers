Zine: Animist Gratitude Prayers
=============

Printable zine of [Scientific Animism: Animist Gratitude Prayers](https://scientificanimism.substack.com/p/animist-gratitude-prayers), made using [lisli/zine-template](https://github.com/lisli/zine-template).

To print, go to [chadoh.com/animist-gratitude-prayers](https://chadoh.com/animist-gratitude-prayers) and print.

- Landscape, not portrait
- Made for US Letter-size paper (feel free to adjust all `in` values in the source for A4 or whatever you need; you might need to bump positions of things around accordingly)
- Print with 0.25in margins on all sides
- Use a paper cutter or scissors to cut off the margins, then fold into a zine:

![How to Fold a Mini Zine: 1. fold hotdog style, 2. fold hamburger style, 3. fold hamburger style again, 4. unfold and check your layout, 5. fold hamburger and cut the middle two panels, 6. unfold again, you should have a hole in the center, 7. fold hotdog again, 8. pop the center panels apart like baow!, 9. fold it up and omg you have a mini zine!](fold.jpg)
